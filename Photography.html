「Exposure Assignment」

As one of our beginning projects the instructor assigned us to go around the school and take pictures
using Shutter mode (Tv) or Exposure mode (A). This helped us get a feel of the camera and what these modes do.

「Adobe Photoshop」

This assignment introduced us to Photoshop filters, We experimented on on pictures we have already taken,
and had the chance to take more around school. This showed us another side of digital photography, editing!

「Color Project」

This project was to get us into the idea of capturing different aspects of lighting, color, boldness etc.
Some examples that we looked for were:

  • Subtility
  • Boldness
  • Brightness
  • Abstract

「Light and Shadow」

An assignment much like the color project, however, this had us focus on just darkness and lighting.
It helped show how we can also use shutter speed to change the picture brightness and/or
finding contrasting areas to take a picture in!

「Favorite Picture」

Just a simple assignment having us upload our favorite picture that we've taken

「Painting with Light」

This is the first assignment we use a specific setting format and tripods in! We looked at some videos as a
group on how to make a light painting with glowsticks, light rods, flashlights, strings, and more!
